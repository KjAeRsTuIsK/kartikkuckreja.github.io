Kartik Kuckreja

Hi! I am a Research Associate at Mohamed bin Zayed University of Artificial Intelligence (MBZUAI), jointly advised by Prof. Ivan Laptev and Prof. Makarand Tapaswi(IIIT-H).

I recently completed my undergrad from Birla Institute of Technology and Sciences, Pilani (BITS-Pilani) in Computer Science. I had the privilege of completing my Undergraduate Thesis (GeoChat) under the guidance of Prof. Fahad Khan, Dr. Salman Khan and Dr. Abhijit Das. Before this, I worked as a Summer Research Intern at IIT Ropar, with Dr. Abhinav Dhall.

Research Interests

I mostly work in the intersection of Deep Learning and Computer Vision, particularly involving Generative AI. Currently working on Long video/ movie understanding (Multimodal) and Embodied AI (Multiagent Robotics). Earlier, I worked on Vision Language Models for Remote Sensing and Deepfakes.
